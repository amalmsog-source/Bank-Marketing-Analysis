Task 4: Key Insights & Business Recommendations¶

Based on the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of the Bank Marketing dataset, we have identified the following 5 key insights:

  1. Impact of Call Duration: Chart #8 and #9 show a strong relationship between duration and success. Longer conversations significantly increase the likelihood of a customer subscribing to a term deposit.
  2. Target Demographics: While "Middle-Aged" customers are the most contacted (Chart #3), "Seniors" and "Young" groups show a higher conversion ratio, suggesting untapped potential in these segments.
  3. Occupation Insights: Chart #4 shows that blue-collar workers and management are the primary targets, but smaller groups like students and retired individuals actually have higher subscription rates relative to their size.
  4. Seasonal Trends: Campaign activity peaks sharply in May (Chart #10). However, the bank should evaluate if this high volume leads to "customer fatigue," as subscription rates don't always scale with call volume.
  5. Financial Health: The box plot (Chart #7) indicates that customers who say "Yes" tend to have a higher median account balance, suggesting that financial stability is a key predictor of interest in term deposits.

Conclusion¶

This project successfully moved from raw data to actionable insights. By focusing on longer engagement times and targeting specific demographic niches like retired individuals, the bank can improve its marketing efficiency.
